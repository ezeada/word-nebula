I make contemporary art with contemporary materials,

and formats and media easily available and widely used today.

I use industrially produced consumer products,

digital technologies and online platforms

in the same way others use photography, video,

oil paint or clay. The themes that run through my work

deal with the origin and cultural impact of these new materials.

I think this is the best way to avoid a categorization

that would put my work in a niche that is perhaps too narrow.

Digital art is a specific stream of art production

that historically has accumulated a certain identity and agency.

Although I was originally inspired by it,

I prefer my work to be seen as just contemporary.
